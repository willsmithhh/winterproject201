How it Works

  • Click and drag from the ball to aim and set power.
  • You can pull back farther now for longer shots.
  • When the ball drops, use the pop-up button to continue.

Drag backward from the ball to aim. Release to shoot. Avoid the bunkers and use the walls if you want a riskier angle.
